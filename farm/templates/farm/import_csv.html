Import Records from CSV

Bring in your own existing daily records in bulk instead of typing them in one at a time.

Before you upload

  • Columns required: date, flock_size, flock_age_weeks, feed_intake_kg, egg_count, temperature_c, humidity_pct — or the equivalent natural names (e.g. "Number of Flocks", "Average Age of Flock (weeks)", "Feed Intake (kgs per day)", "Egg Yield (per day)", "Temperature", "Humidity") if that's what your own spreadsheet already uses. Column order doesn't matter.
  • Dates must be in YYYY-MM-DD format (e.g. 2024-01-01)
  • Flock size: 1–100,000 · Flock age: 1–150 weeks · Feed intake: 0–150 kg · Egg count: 0–1000 · Temperature: 0–45°C · Humidity: 0–100%
  • All rows are checked before anything is saved — if any row has a problem, nothing is imported and every issue is listed below so you can fix your file and re-upload.
