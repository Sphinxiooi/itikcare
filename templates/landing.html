Powerful Features of ItikCare

Everything you need to optimize your itik farm production and profitability.

  • ✓ AI-Powered Forecasting
  • ✓ Decision Support System
  • ✓ Easy Data Logging
  • ✓ Historical Data Analysis
  • ✓ Continuous Learning

Under the hood, ItikCare trains a Random Forest Regression model on your farm's own daily logs to forecast egg yield one to three days ahead, and surfaces which factors — flock age, feed intake, temperature, humidity — most influenced each prediction. A rule-based prescriptive engine then turns those factors into concrete recommendations, and every recommendation is traceable back to the exact variable and rule that triggered it, so you always know why ItikCare is telling you what it's telling you.
